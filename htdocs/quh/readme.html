Because there are way too many audio players, I decided that will replace them all!

supports 100+ different file, stream or data formats that contain noise:
cdda, mp3, ogg, flac, sid (c64 tunes), stdin, wav, xmp (669, ac1d, alm, amd, chn,
crb, di, digi, emod, exo, far, fc-m, fnk, imf, it, kris, ksm, m15, mdl, med, mod,
modl, mp, mtm, mtn, np, okta, pm, pp10, prom, pru, ptm, rad, s3m, sfx, stim, stm,
stx, tp, ult, unic, wn, wow, xann, xm, zen), raw

supports 10+ different ways of producing noise:
alsa, oss, sdl, pc speaker (if present), etc...

Optionally, takes advantage of the following GPL libraries:
TiMidity, audiofile, libsndfile, libxmp, mikmod, sndlib, libsidplay, lirc, libSDL

can be controlled with joysticks or remote controls (lirc).

is also an reference example how to cultivate bloated API's into an old fashioned,
always sufficient, easy learning and very simple open, read, write, close and seek
framework.

Stop wasting your open source energies with writing audio players or will play movies to the music!

development won't stop before the Debian distribution has at least one CD with audio players less.
